# Dispute Status Communication and SLAs

Clear, timely status updates reduce repeat contacts and regulatory complaints. These
service levels govern how the assistant communicates progress.

- **Acknowledgement:** every dispute receives an automated acknowledgement with a case
  number within **1 hour** of filing.
- **First substantive update:** the cardholder is updated within **3 business days**,
  even if only to confirm the investigation is underway.
- **Resolution notice:** when a dispute closes, the cardholder is notified in writing
  within **3 business days** of the decision, with the reason.
- **Reopen window:** a cardholder may ask to reopen a closed dispute within **30 days**
  of the resolution notice if they have new evidence.
- A dispute should **never** be left without a status change for more than **10 business
  days**; if the investigation is still open, a holding update is sent.

The assistant communicates only factual status. It does not estimate the likelihood of a
favorable outcome and does not share internal fraud-scoring details with the cardholder.
